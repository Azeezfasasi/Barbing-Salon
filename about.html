About Us

HOME / ABOUT

AT HAYZED BARBING SALON, WE BELIEVE THAT, AT ITS BEST, A BARBER SHOP IS A DEPENDABLE, TRUSTED NEIGHBORHOOD INSTITUTION.

OUR PRIMARY GOAL IS TO BUILD STRONG, LONG-TERM RELATIONSHIPS WITH EACH OF OUR CLIENTS.

Hayzed Barbing Salon is proud to be an independent and operated store focused solely on serving its customers. Our unparalleled team of expert barbers has personal pride riding on each haircut and hot lather shave. No one on Hayzed Barbers' staff has been cutting hair fewer than 10 years. We've seen it all and can do it all. We are about substance and skill, not gimmicks. We like to have fun, but we take our work seriously.

MEET OUR TEAM

MICHEAL

MASTER BARBER

Micheal — hails from Middlesex County and has had a passion for cutting hair since he was a kid. He honed his craft back in high school where he kept an ever-increasing number of classmates looking sharp. His signature cut is a classic combover with detailed scissor work. Spends free time with family and on the golf course.

BROWN

MASTER BARBER

Brown — a third-generation barber who started cutting hair in Morocco when he was just 13. Very laid-back personality, but holds himself to exacting standards when he’s cutting. Loves to reinterpret and modernize classic cuts that he free-hands to shape the face of each client. He will talk European soccer for hours.

JONHSON

MASTER BARBER

Johnson – moved to the US from Colombia when he was 12. Quiet and focused, he prefers to let his work do the talking. Takes great pleasure in making clients happy and sharpening their look. A certified car-lover, he appreciates both classic models as well as modern sports cars.
